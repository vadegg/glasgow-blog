# glasgow-blog

Blog for `blog.glasgow.works`.

## Content rules

- All blog content is written in English.
- The target audience is US/EU founders, product leaders, B2B SaaS teams, growth teams, and agencies in IT.
- The blog is focused on product research, customer interviews, JTBD, market validation, go-to-market research, and research systems.
- Do not publish content about general management, lifestyle, politics, esoterica, or broad AI hot takes.
